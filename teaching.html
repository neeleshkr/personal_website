Content (as student, pre-TA)

iPython Circuits Notebook: I worked with 2 other students to create a circuits-oriented iPython notebook that showcased `lcapy`, a convenient way to build circuit diagrams and analyze circuit configurations (symbolically and numerically) in iPython. The notebook allows students to better understand circuit behavior. This goes far beyond a simulation tool like Falstad or even Spice; the convenience/usefulness tradeoff is unbeaten by lcapy. For any circuit, you can find the frequency response, cutoff frequencies, a bode plot, the thevenin equivalent, and so much more. Our professor, Anant Sahai took note of our work, awarding us extra credit in the class and recommending us for TA positions next fall.
